Kunwari Landslide (Bageshwar, Uttarakhand)

ISRO obtained satellite images of this region and made the following observations.

POST: CartoSat-3 (18 Nov 2024)
PRE: RS-2 LISS-IV (17 Apr 2024)

(Swipe up or down to see the visualization)

A landslide-dammed lake was discovered near Kunwari village on November 14, 2024.

  • This barrier lake has been observed to intermittently form and drain since 2014.
  • In 2022, the landslide reactivated again, blocking a tributary of the Pindar River in April and June.
  • The reactivation occurred along a pre-existing slope break, a zone of inherent weakness
  • The river channel has experienced a significant length shortening post-landslide.
  • This recurring blockage poses a significant risk to Kunwari village.
  • The increasing lake volume raises concerns of a potential lake outburst due to the seismic activity in the region.
