Kunwari Landslide (Bageshwar, Uttarakhand)

ISRO obtained satellite images of this region and made the following observations.

POST: CartoSat-3 (18 Nov 2024)
PRE: RS-2 LISS-IV (17 Apr 2024)

(Swipe up or down to see the visualization)

A landslide-dammed lake was discovered near Kunwari village on November 14, 2024

  • This barrier lake has been observed to intermittently form and drain since 2014.
  • This recurring blockage poses a significant risk to the Kunwari village.
  • The increasing lake volume raises concerns of a potential lake outburst due to seismic activeness of the region.
